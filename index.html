About Our Web App

Our web app allows you to easily register your two-wheeler vehicle and manage chalans associated with it. Registering your vehicle is simple and secure, and you can add chalans for any violations quickly.

To get started, simply register your vehicle by providing the required details. Once registered, you can log in and manage your vehicle's chalans. Our user-friendly interface makes the entire process seamless.
